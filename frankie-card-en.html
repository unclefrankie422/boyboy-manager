◆ Biography

Frankie Tsang formerly served as a Hong Kong civil servant. He later joined a Hong Kong-listed company and was stationed in Mainland China for 19 years, serving in senior management and representing the group in handling external public relations and all legal affairs.

After retirement, he founded his own company and established "Xiao Xin Ni Ni" (小心逆逆) Parent Grand Lecture Hall, which has spread across Hong Kong, Macau, and Shenzhen, earning the love and recognition of parents and educators. He is also a qualified service provider for the "Shenzhen People's Livelihood Micro-Projects" initiative, delivering services to multiple communities, schools, and enterprises in Shenzhen. To date, he has accumulated over 28 years of practical experience.
